# Ethical Use Guidelines for InfinityTrace

**InfinityTrace** is an open-source OSINT tool designed for **research, awareness, and digital safety**. It uses **only publicly available information** to generate digital footprint insights.

By using this tool, you agree to follow these ethical guidelines:

---

## ⚖️ Ethical Principles

1. **Public Data Only**  
   Never attempt to access private, restricted, or paywalled content.

2. **No Identity Confirmation**  
   The tool generates **risk indicators** and public footprints only. Do **not** claim ownership of any account.

3. **No Harassment or Doxxing**  
   Results must **not** be used to harass, stalk, or target anyone.

4. **Transparency & Consent**  
   Only analyze data that the user has **provided voluntarily** or is **publicly accessible**.

5. **Responsible Sharing**  
   Do **not** redistribute reports or information that could harm individuals.

6. **Legal Compliance**  
   Follow all local laws and regulations regarding online research and data privacy.

---

## ⚠️ Limitations & Responsibility

- **False Positives:** Public data may be incomplete or outdated; always use caution.  
- **No Guarantees:** InfinityTrace does **not guarantee the accuracy** of any report.  
- **User Responsibility:** Users are responsible for how they interpret and use the generated reports.

---

By using InfinityTrace, you agree to abide by these guidelines. Misuse of the tool **for illegal or harmful purposes** may result in legal consequences.

**Trace signals, not people.**
